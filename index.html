01 · Context
Why Organic Channels No Longer Work

Organic recruitment in niche segments like CDL and owner-operators produces virtually no results. Competition for drivers has intensified sharply, and candidates typically apply to 5–10 companies simultaneously. Whoever reaches out first wins the hire.

Paid channels are the only tool that allows systematic, predictable control over the applicant pipeline. A well-structured funnel combining Facebook/Meta Ads + Indeed + TikTok/Reels fundamentally transforms both the volume and quality of incoming candidates.

This proposal outlines three ad spend tiers with projected outcomes, along with the content materials the company provides to ensure an effective launch.
